An authentic art of living... Beaumier creates places for feeling well. Singular destinations enriched by the landscapes and places that surround and nourish them. Inspiring experiences and events combine with authenticity and comfort, minimalism and generosity, the past, present and future. They awaken emotions and create memories to keep.

SOULFUL LUXURY HOTELS

For discerning travellers, the word Beaumier rolls off the tongue. Familiar, yet distinctive, the name we share with a 19th century explorer makes a lasting impression - just like our properties. Far from the hustle and bustle of the city, they are glorious places where memorable experiences make every moment extraordinary. Peaceful and unspoiled locations, locally inspired food and drink, and perfectly relaxing spas and wellness - it's time to discover Beaumier's new vision of luxury.
